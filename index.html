Our MP and MSP

Alyn Smith

MP for Stirling

Our MP is Alyn Smith who was elected in December 2019. Prior to becoming MP for Stirling, Alyn was one of Scotland’s three SNP MEPs in the European parliament. At Westminster he is the shadow SNP Spokesperson (Foreign and Commonwealth Office)

Tel: : 01786 471899

Email: alyn.smith.mp@parliament.uk

OFFICE: 16, Stirling Enterprise Park (STEP), Stirling, FK7 7RP

Keith Brown

MSP for Clackmannanshire and Dunblane

Our MSP is Keith Brown. Keith has been an MSP since 2007, when he was first elected for the Ochil constituency. Keith stood down from the Scottish Government in 2018 to focus on his role as deputy leader of the Scottish National Party

Tel: 01259 219333

Email: keith.brown.msp@parliament.scot

OFFICE: Unit 4, 39 Drysdale Street, Alloa, FK10 1LY
